Tips

What you can do to go greener

Composting

Composting is a great way of putting your waste to good use and helping out the local wildlife as well. Keep your cardboard, raw fruit and veggie scraps and start your pile!

Freecycling

Want to get a new appliance but don't want to buy something new? Why not get onto your local freecycling pages and exchange goods with your neighbours.

Community programmes

Choose from community gardens, eco-builds, volunteer schemes and more.
